Espresso

Espresso is a strong, concentrated coffee made by forcing hot water through finely ground coffee beans. It is the base for many coffee drinks, such as cappuccinos and lattes.

Latte

A latte is a coffee drink made with espresso and steamed milk. It is usually topped with a layer of frothed milk.

Cappuccino

A cappuccino is a coffee drink made with espresso, steamed milk, and frothed milk. It is typically served in a small cup.
